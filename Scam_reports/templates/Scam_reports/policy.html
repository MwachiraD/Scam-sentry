Policy

This policy explains what content is allowed and how takedown requests are handled.

What you can report

  • Fraudulent phone numbers, accounts, or identities.
  • Impersonation attempts and phishing campaigns.
  • Suspicious payment requests or crypto scams.

What you should not post

  • Personal information (addresses, national IDs, bank details).
  • Harassment, hate speech, or threats.
  • Unverifiable accusations against private individuals.

Takedown requests

If a report is inaccurate or contains sensitive data, use the “Report issue” link on that report. We review submissions quickly.

Privacy

We store reports and metadata to help protect the community. Contact us for data removal requests.
